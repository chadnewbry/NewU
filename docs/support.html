Frequently Asked Questions

Is my data private?

Yes. All data is stored locally on your device and optionally synced via your personal iCloud account. We never collect or access your data. See our Privacy Policy.

How do I delete my data?

Go to Settings → Clear All Data to remove all app data. Uninstalling the app also removes local data. To clear iCloud data, disable iCloud sync for NewU in your device settings.

How do I restore my purchase?

Go to Settings → Restore Purchase. Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID used for the original purchase.

Can I request a refund?

Refunds are handled by Apple. Visit reportaproblem.apple.com to request a refund.

Does NewU provide medical advice?

No. NewU is a personal tracking tool only. Always consult your healthcare provider for medical decisions.
