Privacy Policy

Effective: February 28, 2026

Chad Newbry, LLC ("we," "us," or "our") built NewU as a personal health-tracking app. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information.

Information We Collect

NewU stores the following data locally on your device and, if enabled, synced via your personal iCloud account:

  • Profile information (height, weight, goals)
  • Injection logs (medication, dosage, injection site, date)
  • Weight entries
  • Nutrition logs (protein, fiber, calories, water intake)
  • Side effect reports
  • Daily notes
  • Workout summaries

Apple HealthKit

If you grant permission, NewU reads and writes health data (weight, steps, workouts) via Apple HealthKit. This data is governed by Apple's HealthKit policies and is never sent to our servers or shared with third parties. HealthKit data is not used for advertising.

Data Storage & Sync

All data is stored on-device using SwiftData. If iCloud is enabled on your device, data syncs through your personal iCloud account using Apple's CloudKit framework. We do not operate external servers and do not have access to your iCloud data.

In-App Purchases

NewU offers a one-time purchase for full access, processed entirely through Apple's App Store. We do not collect or store payment information.

Analytics & Tracking

NewU does not include any third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking frameworks. We do not collect usage analytics.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, share, or transmit your personal data to any third party. Your health data stays on your device and in your personal iCloud account.

Data Deletion

You can delete all app data at any time from Settings → Clear All Data. Uninstalling the app removes all local data. To remove iCloud data, disable iCloud sync for NewU in your device settings.

Children's Privacy

NewU is not intended for use by children under 17. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
